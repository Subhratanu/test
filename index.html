About

The International Conference on Smart Technologies for Sustainable Development (ICSTSD2021) is to be organized to address various issues of Smart Technologies to prosper the creation of intelligent solutions in future. The aim is to bring together worldwide leading researchers, developers, practitioners and educators interested in advancing the state of the art in various engineering disciplines for exchanging knowledge that encompasses a broad range of research area among various distinct communities. It is expected that researchers will bring new prospect for collaboration across disciplines and gain idea facilitating novel breakthrough. The theme for this conference is the inculcation of innovations in smart technologies for holistic development and implementation for societal benefit.
The conference will provide an exceptional platform to the researchers to meet and discuss the solutions of recent investigations, scientific results and various contemporary issues with people that actively involved in these evergreen fields. The 2-days conference commencing on 28th – 29th October 2021, will feature reputed keynote speakers, tutorials, Panel Discussions. All accepted and presented papers will be submitted to Scopus indexed journals for Publication.
ICSTSD2021 will no doubt be proven to be exciting and knowledge sharing platform. The organizing team cordially invite you to take part in this upcoming event and together we flourish it into a most memorable experience.
